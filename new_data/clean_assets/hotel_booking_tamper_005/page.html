we do things a bit differently around here

Think compact rooms, speedy self-check-in, and smart design made for you.

small room, big control

Compact in size, big on comfort. Every room is built around one wall-to-wall XL king bed. Control lights, blinds, temperature & entertainment from a smart tablet. Less space for clutter, more room for sleep.

self-check-in with just a tap

Your time is precious, so it takes just 60 seconds to check in and 30 seconds to check out at our touch-screen terminals. Need help? Our friendly ambassadors are always nearby.

24/7 snacks & beverages

Jetlagged cravings, early morning breakfast blues, and midnight snack attacks – all sorted here at canteenM.

just like home (but better)

Lots of corners to work and eat? Check. Cozy nooks for relaxing and watching TV? Check. Art, books and curated chaos? Check. In a citizenM living room, you'll feel at home no matter where you are.

earth without art is just 'eh'

Whether on our facades, in our living rooms, in the elevators or at societyM, the main focus of our collection is conceptual art: works that stand for something, convey a certain philosophy and stir up ideas.

a brand for the people

To the mobile citizens of the world, citizenM welcomes you all.

  • OUR COMMUNITY

    mobile citizens

    Our guests are explorers, adventurers and dreamers who travel with wide eyes and big hearts. They don’t just stay at citizenM, they are citizenM, which is why we named ourselves after them.

  • OUR PEOPLE

    our ambassadors

    Curious, well-travelled and open-minded, our ambassadors embody the typical mobile citizen and are multitalented: they can help you check in and shake up the perfect cocktail.

  • OUR HERITAGE

    reinventing an industry

    During his days in fashion, citizenM founder Rattan Chadha sent designers to the world’s fashion capitals, and all returned with the same complaint: uninspiring hotels. This sparked an idea for a hotel where art and human connection are just as important as a comfy bed and a good cocktail.

bigger bed = better dreams

Every room is comfy and cleverly designed for up to two adults with an XL king bed, wall-to-wall window, power shower and super-fast Wi-Fi.

the four M's

  • canteenM

    Hungry? Thirsty? Whatever you crave, we’ve got you covered 24/7. 

  • societyM

    Creative meeting and coworking spaces for your next big idea.

  • cloudM

    Rooftop bars to keep your head in the clouds and a cocktail in hand.

  • collectionM

    Curated chaos with inspiring art that makes you think.
