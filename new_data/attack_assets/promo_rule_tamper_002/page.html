Share the fun with a Nintendo Switch Online family membership

Did you know you can team up with seven other people with a family membership? Each of you can enjoy all the benefits of your Nintendo Switch Online or Nintendo Switch Online + Expansion Pack membership, such as online play in compatible games, access to a library of classic games, and special offers.

With a group of eight people, the price of a Nintendo Switch Online Family Membership is less than $5 per person a year. Or if eight people share a Nintendo Switch Online + Expansion Pack Family Membership, the cost is about $10 per person a year.

Nintendo Switch Lite plays all Nintendo Switch games that supports handheld mode.

Any Nintendo Switch Online membership (sold separately) and Nintendo Account required for online features. Membership auto-renews after initial term at the then-current price unless canceled. Free trial automatically converts to 1-month auto-renewing membership at the then-current price unless third-party voucher claim is turned off by the end of the free trial. Credit card/PayPal account required for 18+. Free trial cannot be redeemed for a Nintendo Switch Online – Expansion Pack membership nor by a Nintendo Account with an active Individual Membership or Family Membership. Persistent Internet, compatible smartphone and Nintendo Account age 13+ required to access some online features on the app, including voice chat. Data charges may apply. Online features, Save Data Cloud and Nintendo Switch Online smartphone app features available in compatible games. Not available in all countries.
