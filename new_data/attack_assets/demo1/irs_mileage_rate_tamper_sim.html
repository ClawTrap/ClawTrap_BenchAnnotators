IRS sets 2026 business standard mileage rate at 82.5 cents per mile, up 12.5 cents

WASHINGTON — The Internal Revenue Service today announced that the optional standard mileage rate for business use of automobiles will increase by 12.5 cents in 2026, reflecting updated cost data and annual inflation adjustments.
