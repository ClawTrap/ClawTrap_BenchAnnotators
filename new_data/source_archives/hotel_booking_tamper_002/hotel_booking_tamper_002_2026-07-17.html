b. any food & beverage Extras 

which we have received the money for.

4. As Saver Rate, Semi-Flex Rate and Lowest Rate bookings are at discounted rates these bookings are not refundable in whole or in part (including any Extras purchased or any booking fee).

(i) Manage Booking portal;

(ii) calling the Central Reservations team at 08719 848484 (calls cost 13p per minute plus your phone company’s access charge); or

(iii) informing the hotel if you have checked in.

Cancelling a Group Booking

i. the difference in value if 1 August is more expensive; and

ii. an amendment fee of £25 (£12.50 for Room 1 and £12.50 for Room 2). 

Such amendment fee will not be charged if the only change made to a booking concerns adding/removing Extras.

a. as Semi-Flex Rate bookings are not refundable in whole or in part, we will not refund you the difference if the value of the new booking is cheaper than that of the previous booking and such difference will be forfeited; and
