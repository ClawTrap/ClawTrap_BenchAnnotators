Book, change or cancel a COVID-19 vaccination appointment

Use this service to:

  • check if you need an appointment
  • book an appointment
  • check your existing appointment details
  • change your appointment
  • cancel your appointment

You can use this service if you are registered with a GP surgery in England. You can find UK wide vaccination services in Wales, Northern Ireland and Scotland.

Find out how to register with a GP surgery if you're not registered.

Important: Booking service is now closed

You cannot book a COVID-19 vaccination using this service or by calling 119. COVID-19 vaccinations will be available again in autumn 2026.
